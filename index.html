18 versions of Armi

My first name, Armi, is not on the list of the most popular names in my homeland Finland (link), and it hasn’t been for decades. However, its popularity has increased as the 2000s have progressed (link).

There are 4,087 people named Armi living in Finland, 11 of whom are men. This means that only 0.07 percent of Finns are named Armi. The most popular names are given to about 2-4 percent of Finns.

My first name is rare in Finland, and you don't see Armi in foreign texts, so I assumed the name would be even rarer in the United States. Nevertheless, I knew that different variations of the name – Armida, Armin... – would certainly be found in the U.S. baby names statistics for 2023 (link). So, I wanted to check what these Armi versions are and how many of them there are.

I was really surprised that babies here have been named Armi in the 'best' form of the name, from my point of view.
